About Our Society

How everything started

The Suketcine M.P. Co-operative society was formed in the year 1959.

It is formed with the vision to serve the people of Sundernagar to entertain them so that they can relax in no time . This Society became the most famous and Reputed Society in whole Himachal Pradesh and the vision to keep succeeding in the financial sector made this the most Profitable & Prestigious Society of all other Competition. Since starting, we are leading market’s centre of attraction and we believe we are just getting warmed up for our future.
